Leif Oltedal is a consultant in neuroradiology (Haukeland University Hospital) and Associate Professor of Radiology (University of Bergen, Norway). His background is from basic neuroanatomy and patch-clamp electrophysiology (PhD-2009). After starting training to become a radiologist he engaged in quantitative neuroradiological imaging of ECT both as a postdoc (2013-17) and as Fulbright visiting scholar to UC San Diego (2016-17).
